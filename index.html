London

London is the capital of the United Kingdom

known for its rich history, iconic landmarks like Big Ben and the Tower of London, and its status as a global financial and cultural hub.

Paris

Paris is the capital of France

famous for its iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and Louvre Museum, as well as its rich history, art, and fashion.

Tokyo

Tokyo is the capital of Japane

known for its blend of ultramodern technology and traditional culture, iconic landmarks like the Tokyo Tower, and its status as one of the world's largest and most vibrant cities.
